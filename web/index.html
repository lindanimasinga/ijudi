IJudi for customers

Do you always need to withdraw cash everytime you want to buy from your local shops? yes. You can load your cash into a digital wallet from any Pick n Pay, Spar or from any ATM. Order all your favourite meals from IJudi Market without a need of a bank card.



IJudi for my shop

Before you even know about how Ijudi works. You are ready to receive online orders. You just need a cellphone number, create your iJudi profile, add your stock and start receiving orders and cash to your digital wallet. You can withdraw your funds at any Pick n Pay, Spar or at any ATM.
